Who we are

AseeksB is a place to openly share what you’re looking for. Whatever kind, type, or style of relationship it is you’re looking for. Even Missed Connections.

It’s a place to express yourself. To communicate what’s important to you.

It’s a place where transparency and authenticity are appreciated and encouraged. Where differences are valued. And where you can feel secure in the knowledge that the needs and desires you’ve chosen to express are only being displayed to those open to seeing them revealed.

It’s a place that respects your right to not only control who’s able to see your post but also who can reach out to you.

It’s a place that values your time. That believes in accountability. That'll do everything it can to help you avoid wasting time on posts that aren’t looking for what you want, or worse, that aren’t really looking for anything at all.

We’re a place that believes that you’re worth more than a pic’s milli-second pause. That you’re worth listening to. That your words are worth reading. Worth consideration.

We’re AseekB. Real people. Real posts. We’re post-swipe. Post what you seek.

Transparency

Some sites and apps try to hide their users. Like nightclubs with entrances concealed by blackout curtains try to ask you to “buy a ticket” before you actually know who’s inside – we don’t do that here – our search results display the number of posts returned. If you hide a post or block a poster the counter on the bottom-right will automatically update to reflect that.

Accountability

We’re serious about your safety, privacy, and time. At AseeksB we make it incredibly easy to report profiles, posts, or messages that don’t meet our guidelines. You can report message threads even after they’ve been unmatched. This means no hit and runs. No “anglerfish” bait-and-switches. Reported posts get removed from all users’ Bulletin Boards, so your actions help others too.

Features


Searching and Messaging Features

  • Hide posts: If you’re not into a post, hide it. It’ll say hidden even if the poster renews it
  • Hide profiles: If you’re not feeling a poster, you can hide all of their posts – no matter what category they’ve been posted in. This also hides all of your posts from them, so they won’t be able to message you either
  • Be Real: If you believe someone is misrepresenting themselves in order to circumvent your search criteria, report them. Any such attempt will lead to immediate action
  • Location: There is no “Travel Mode.” No way to inflate the amount of results a location has. Or to waste your time when you’re looking to meet someone local. A poster’s location must meet your search criteria. If it’s not, report it
  • Conversation: No more one-word self-summaries, nor profiles that are only advertising social media. Posts have a minimum character requirement. And have to meet our Post Guidelines
  • Photos: You can choose to require pics of the poster. And we don’t allow irrelevant photos. See our Photo Guidelines for more info


Posting Features

  • One Initial Message Per Post: Users can’t message you again until you reply. That means thoughtful messages. And no pestering
  • Renew: Posts can be renewed every 2 weeks. Renew yours to hop to the top of the list
  • Multiple: We understand that people can want more than one thing at once, so up to 3 posts are allowed every 2 weeks
  • Viewers: Choose your audience. You get to choose who gets to see you
  • Be Real: If you believe someone is misrepresenting themselves in order to circumvent your post’s criteria, report them. Any such attempt will lead to immediate action
  • Location: There is no “Travel Mode.” A messenger’s location must meet your post’s criteria. If it’s not, report it
  • Conversation: No more one-word, “Hi, Hey, Wyd,” initial messages. Initial messages have minimum character requirements, and they need to meet our Message Guidelines
  • Photos: There’s no requirement to attach photos to posts, but if a poster does, they have to be of the poster. See our Photo Guidelines for more info

Frequently Asked Questions

When will you be available in my awesome city/state/country?
We’re working on it! Join Us at Launch and let us know where you’d like us to be!
Why isn’t there a travel mode? Why can’t I message someone in a different city?
We deeply believe in respecting someone’s search or posting criteria. Location among them. If they’re in a city and are looking to meet, please be in that city
Will you ever support couples accounts?
It’s on our to-do list. Contact us through the app if you’d like additional features!
Can I change my post’s font?
We currently only support regular text, but reach out to us if you’d like additional features!
Will you support additional filtering features?
Yes. See our Coming Features. If you have tags or hashtags you’d like to use, contact us through the app to let us know
Can I create a post for an event?
Not currently. Please do not post an event as it will get an account banned. When we do though, we’ll also update a user’s search filters so they can avoid those posts if they want to. It’s a user’s right to choose what kind of posts they see
Why are social media handles not allowed to be advertised on posts?
  • Unfortunately the vast majority of social media handle references in app self-summaries are there to serve as a follower-funnels, or worse are there to lure users to another service where they’ll be propositioned/scammed/have their time wasted. Therefore, to protect our users, social media handles in posts are not allowed
  • Addendum: If after messaging for a bit you’d like to exchange social media handles, that’s perfectly fine, but please review our Message Guidelines, and please remember that message threads can be reported even after they’ve been disconnected

Privacy

We deeply believe in the right to privacy. Especially when it comes to one’s needs and desires. AseeksB does not collect or track information that’s not part of industry norms.
